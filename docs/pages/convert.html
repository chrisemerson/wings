Converts numbers between different units

Available Units: DISTANCE CM - Centimetre FT - Foot IN - Inch KM - Kilometre M - Metre MI - Mile MM - Millimetre NM - Nautical Mile YD - Yard WEIGHT G - Gram KG - Kilogram LB - Pound OZ - Ounce ST - Stone TONS - Ton TONNES - Tonne VOLUME CC - Cubic Centimetre CFT - Cubic Foot CIN - Cubic Inch CM - Cubic Metre CMM - Cubic Millimetre GAL - Gallon L - Litre ML - Millilitre OZ - Fluid Ounce PT - Pint QT - Quart USGAL - US Gallon USOZ - US Fluid Ounce USPT - US Pint USQT - US Quart ANGLES DEG - Degree RAD - Radian SPEED C - Speed Of Light KT - Knot KPH - Kilometres Per Hour MPH - Miles Per Hour MPS - Metres Per Second PRESSURE ATM - Atmosphere BAR - Bar INHG - Inches Of Mercury MB - Millibar PSI - Pounds Per Square Inch TEMPERATURE C - Celcius F - Farenheit K - Kelvin Usage: Convert::UNIT1toUNIT2(value); Eg, to convert 54km to miles: Convert::KMtoMI(54);
